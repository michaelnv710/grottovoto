Ethics

Secrecy

Throwing that information around tends to eventually add difficulty to entering and less enjoyment while inside, because people bring attention and wear. Take Only Pictures

We do this for the fun and adventure, not profit. I don't think many people set out exploring in search of loot, so most theft that results is probably simply of opportunity. Whatsoever, it's unacceptable. We don't want people stealing our stuff, so we don't steal theirs. If you still feel inclined to take what isn't yours, let me mention that robbery is much worse than trespassing should you be caught.

Leave Only Footprints

The two phrases are seperated to emphasize that you don't need to take something from a site to degrade it. Some of the nefarious tomfoolery includes breaking and entering, vandalism, graffiti, municipal and bodily dumping, arsen, et cetera. You get the idea, just respect urban environments with the same respect as other natural environments like the forest.

Couth

Our ethics didn't feel complete without this one. Just don't be stupid, have some courtesy, and have some fun. Don't be a brick and lock up entrances or steal stories. Please don't add to the list of utterly retarded graffiti that we find a lot of (link).

Minnesota Statutes

609.605 Trespass

Goes over the basics, it's pretty self-explanatory.

609.6055 Trespass on Criical Public Service Facility

This protects quite a lot of facilites, it mainly applies to drains and bridges for us though. Note the differences between aboveground and underground misdemeanors. Above, you can legally leave when asked to, unless signs are posted or if they've already asked you to leave in the last six months. But below, it's automatically a misdemeanor even without postings. Also, employees of the facility can detain you (link to Ford kidnapping) with reasonable cause, and you can be arrested on probably cause of violation in the preceding four hours.

97A.315 Trespass

If you knowingly disregard a sign, a personally notification, or commit three misdmemeanors in one year, they turn gross.

92.70 Land Use Trespass

Basically just adds responsibility to pay two or three times any damages, if you're into that.

97B.001 Trespass

You can retrieve a hunting dog without permission, and you must leave the gate how you found it.

609.153 Increased Penalties for Certain Misdemeanors

You can get searched, like you didn't know that. Three strikes and you get gross misdemeanors too.
